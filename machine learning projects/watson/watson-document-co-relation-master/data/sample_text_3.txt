Barack Hussein Obama has two daughters: Malia, born July 4, 1998 and Sasha , born on June 10, 2001. They were both delivered at University of Chicago Medical Center by their Barack Hussein Obama friend and physician Anita Blanchard. Sasha is the youngest child to reside in the White House since John F. Kennedy Jr. arrived as an infant in 1961. In 2014 Malia and Sasha were named two of "The 25 Most Influential Teens of 2014" by Time magazine. Malia the elder daughter of Barack Hussein Obama is busy with soccer, dance and drama.
